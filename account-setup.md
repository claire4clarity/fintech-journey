## Tutorial - Create a Bank Account

Welcome to Hermes FG!  To get started with our banking services, you will need to complete the following setup.

1. *Create Your Profile:* Enter your email and a strong password to begin.  It cannot be any part of your name or birthdate to make the password more secure.
2. *Identification:* Upload a photo of your government ID, which is a driver’s license, passport, or state ID.  This is to protect you from identity theft.
3. *Choose Your Account Type:* Select a Checking Account for daily spending, a Savings Account for short-term savings, or a CD for long-term savings.  Each account has a competitive interest rate to help you meet your money goals.
4. *Connect to Another Bank Account:* A deposit of $5 is required to open an account, so you will need to link your profile to a funding source to complete a transfer.
5. *Secure Your Account:* Set up “Two-Factor Authentication” through text or the Hermes FG app to make sure that only you can access your funds.
