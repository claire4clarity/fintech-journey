##ChronosVault  Quick Guide

**Caution:** Banks are closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and Federal Holidays. The transaction will be completed on the next banking day.

Let Hermes FG handle remembering to schedule transfers for savings or loan repayments. You can schedule transfers for up to ten years and let us take care of the rest.

1. Click on ChronosVault: Found in the Accounts drop-down menu.
2. Select Vendor or Account:
3. Enter Transaction Details: Enter the amount and the banking date you want the money to reach the account.
4. Click on Schedule: Complete the transaction and save it in our system.

*Want it to be a recurring transaction?*  Check the “Recurring” checkbox right above the Schedule button.
